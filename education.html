EDUCATION BACKGROUND

My six-year journey at Sekolah Kebangsaan Gangsa from 2010 to 2015 holds a special place in my heart. The nurturing and inclusive environment provided a foundation for both academic and personal growth. The dedicated teachers and staff played a pivotal role in my development, ensuring a quality education and providing unwavering support.

Lifelong friendships were forged during this time, creating a sense of belonging and camaraderie. Beyond academics, the school emphasized character development, instilling values of integrity, responsibility, and empathy. Co-curricular activities and community projects taught the importance of teamwork and leadership. Reflecting on my time at Sekolah Kebangsaan Gangsa, I am grateful for the experiences and knowledge gained.

The education received laid a solid foundation for future pursuits, nurturing a love for learning and a thirst for knowledge. The school's nurturing environment and guidance continue to inspire me to strive for excellence. Sekolah Kebangsaan Gangsa will always hold a special place in my heart, as it shaped my character and set me on a path of personal and academic success.
My secondary school journey at Sekolah Menengah Arab JAIM Assyakirin from 2016 to 2020 was a transformative period despite the challenges brought by the COVID-19 pandemic, which resulted in an early graduation in 2021. This esteemed institution provided a unique educational experience rooted in Arabic language, Islamic studies, and cultural heritage. The dedicated teachers and staff played a vital role in nurturing my academic growth and instilling discipline and perseverance.

The bonds formed with my classmates were a source of support and camaraderie as we navigated our studies together. Despite the unforeseen circumstances, Sekolah Menengah Arab JAIM Assyakirin adapted swiftly, providing remote learning opportunities and continued support.

Reflecting on my secondary school years, I am grateful for the comprehensive education and diverse experiences gained. The values, knowledge, and skills acquired have prepared me for the future. Sekolah Menengah Arab JAIM Assyakirin will always hold a special place in my heart as a pivotal institution that contributed to my personal and academic development.
My diploma journey at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kedah has been a transformative experience since I began at the age of 18 in 2021. As I approach the end of my diploma, set to conclude in July 2023, I reflect on the invaluable knowledge and skills I have acquired in the field of library science, specifically in the IM120 course.

Studying at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kedah has provided me with a comprehensive education in the library field. The dedicated lecturers and supportive staff have been instrumental in guiding my academic growth and professional development. Their expertise and mentorship have enabled me to navigate the intricacies of library science and prepare for a rewarding career in this field.

Throughout my diploma journey, I have had the opportunity to engage in hands-on learning experiences and practical training. The university's well-equipped library facilities have allowed me to gain practical knowledge in cataloging, information retrieval, and managing library resources. These experiences have enhanced my understanding of the importance of efficient library services and the impact they have on information dissemination and knowledge sharing.

Moreover, studying alongside my fellow students has been a source of inspiration and motivation. Collaborative projects, discussions, and shared experiences have fostered a sense of camaraderie and teamwork among us, creating a supportive learning environment.
